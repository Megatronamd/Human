The Lazy Cat

I had a cat, so round and fat,
He slept all day upon my mat.
He dreamed of fish and flying mice,
But woke up just to eat some rice.

Toothpaste Tango

I squeezed the tube with all my might,
It shot across the room in flight!
It hit the wall, then hit my shoe—
Now minty fresh from heel to toe too!

Wi-Fi Woes

My Wi-Fi died, I screamed in fear,
“How will I scroll or stream or cheer?”
I stared at walls, I paced the floor—
Then read a book... what a bore!
